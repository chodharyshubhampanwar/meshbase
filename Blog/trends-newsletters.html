The Newsletter Revolution


Introduction

Content is truly becoming the king and with new age customers, it is important for every creator to be digital first. Due to the intenet revolution in india, more and more people are consuming content everyday and even paying for the quality and exclusive community based creators and this trend has only been moving upwards but can it survive with big players and media houses coming after this market.

Problem

  1. Fake news. Social media companies have yet to find ways to prevent the spread of fake news on their platforms.
  2. Digital and Offline Mainstream Media Channels; Platforms in India are full of Low-quality content with clickbaity headlines and full of annoying Ads.

Trends

  • Once considered only to be creative weekend projects, the pandemic has accelerated the status of newsletters to be viable sources of income.
  • The concept of Newsletters in India is fairly new but the tide is slowly turning in the favour of Independent Players and Content Creators.
  • Consumers are ready to pay for Premium, Relevant, and well-Researched content.
  • Freelancers are translating their brands into direct income.

Moving forward

  • Write for your readers. Not advertisers.
  • Paid Newsletters will help in filtering Noise from the Content.
  • Members are more engaged.
  • Email digests serve as a bridge between the reader and the publication. For some readers, they have already replaced publishers as the primary source of news.

Current Players

The Juggernaut The-Ken Finshots The Morning Context The Alipore Post Splainer

Opportunities

  • Building an India First Platform for 1.4 Billion price conscious Indian Consumers is not easy but a well researched product will find its buyers and users.
  • A Medium that allows creators to easily publish content with limited friction. A Shopify for Creators, Publishers that allows them to customise everything.
  • Freemium model combining subscription and Relevant ads.
