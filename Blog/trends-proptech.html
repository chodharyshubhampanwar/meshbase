India’s real estate sector opportunity


Introduction

The global real estate industry has undergone a tectonic shift in the last decade. India’s real estate industry, too, is experiencing the effects of this global disruption. As per the BEF January 2019 report, by 2030, the real estate sector in India is expected to have a market value of USD 1 trillion. And, it will contribute to 13per cent of the country’s total GDP by 2025.

  2. Project finance servicing cost, continuous depletion of margins, increased cost of acquiring a customer, and extended transaction timeline.

Trends

Real estate players have started to rely heavily on new technologies like artificial intelligence, IoT, blockchain and virtual reality. Virtual tour of properties, for instance, is a major technology trend that saves one the hassle of dealing with pesky customers. AI-based chatbots have enabled real estate companies to offer round-the-clock assistance to customers. Smart-building tech is another area of PropTech that is causing disruptions in the real estate sector. The number of co-living space providers in the country has skyrocketed, Catering primarily to students and young working professionals.
